About the server:

Based MC its a cracked anarchy server focused on being close to vanilla with no gameplay restrictions.

It was created on 31, Jully, 2023 its hosted in the EU and the world will never be reset. The world is already over 80GB and over 2000 players joined.

Our community members are mostly over the age of 15 and from europe.
Admins will not mess with normal gameplay unless something breaks, or we get a report of a critical issue caused by a bug or a player.

Our server is aimed for europeans over the age of 14 and we avoid comparing Based MC to other anarchy servers.

If you want to join an active anarchy server that actually carres about the community, Based MC is allways open for you!

/home and /tpa:

Although some people might not consider a server with /home and /tpa anarchy, i certanly do so as this is not a generic 2b2t clone and this doesnt really affect traveling or general gameplay.

These were added because the server was made for groups of people to play together and having 5 homes and the ability to /tpa makes it easier to focus on other aspects of the game like building, PVP, griefing and just hanging with others.
